# inside an explicitly marked legacy section (an `<!-- LEGACY:START -->` … `<!-- LEGACY:END -->` span, or

# depth). Any hit outside a legacy span fails `.aar-ci/skill_consistency_check.sh`.

# `#`-prefixed lines and blank lines are ignored. Add a phrase here once its concept is fully retired
# (superseded terminology that must never resurface unmarked) — see agentic-engineering#51 for the
# incident (a supposedly-retired phrase reappearing) this denylist exists to catch at doc-time.
